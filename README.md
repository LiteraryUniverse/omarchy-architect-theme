# Architect

A theme for those who build worlds with precision — the drafting table, the blueprint, the steel-and-glass tower rising
against a midnight sky. Pure Art Deco at its most architectural: obsidian black, burnished gold, and the cold gleam of
polished chrome. Every border is a structural element. Every corner is a deliberate ornament. Every line is load-bearing.

Where Codex evokes the bound volume, Architect evokes the building that houses the library — the grand foyer, the
gilded elevator cage, the geometric mosaic floor. Borders are doubled and cornered like the frames of a Chrysler Building
window. Buttons are hexagonal capsules with diamond end-caps.

Designed for writers and creators who think in systems, structures, and grand designs.

## Color Philosophy

- **Background**: Pure obsidian (`#0E0D0D`) — the blueprint paper, inverted
- **Foreground**: Warm platinum (`#EDE8E0`) — the drafting pencil line
- **Primary accent**: Champagne gold (`#C9A84C`) — the gilded ornament, structural
- **Secondary accent**: LU pink (`#FF83D5`) — the neon sign above the entrance
- **Tertiary accent**: Chrome silver (`#A8B8C8`) — polished steel, cool and precise
- **Error / warning**: Signal red — the emergency exit sign

## Art Direction

Art Deco at its most architectural: doubled borders with ornamental corner brackets (pseudo-element L-shapes),
geometric diamond corner accents, zero border-radius on most elements. Crisp slide animations, no organic curves.
